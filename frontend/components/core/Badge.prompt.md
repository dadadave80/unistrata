Compact uppercase mono label for status and metadata; tranche-tinted, with a pulsing `live` dot for autonomous Reactive-Network events.

```jsx
<Badge variant="senior">Senior</Badge>
<Badge variant="live" live>Reactive · live</Badge>
<Badge variant="negative" dot>Impaired</Badge>
```

- `variant`: `neutral`, `senior`, `junior`, `positive`, `negative`, `live`.
- `live` adds a pulsing dot (stilled under reduced-motion); `dot` adds a static one.
- `size`: `sm` / `md`.
